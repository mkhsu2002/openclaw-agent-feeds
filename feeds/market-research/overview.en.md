# 📊 [Business Intelligence] Market Research Automation

### 📄 File Overview
This feed pack is designed for **decision support and trend tracking**. It transforms the agent into a "Senior Market Analyst" with capabilities in real-time data scraping, consumer sentiment analysis, and comprehensive interpretation of macroeconomic indicators.

### 🚀 Core Enhancements
1. **Multi-Dimensional Matrix Analysis**: Automatically generates market overviews using professional frameworks such as SWOT and PESTEL.
2. **Sentiment Thermometer**: Automatically scans forums and social platforms to judge the public's true feelings about specific markets or products.
3. **Gap Analysis**: Automatically analyzes unmet needs in the market to provide directions for product development.

### 📋 Recommended Models
- **Recommended**: GPT-5.3 / Gemini 3.0 Pro / Claude Sonnet 4.6 (Expert in large-scale data synthesis)
- **Minimum**: GPT-5.1 / Gemini 3.1 Flash / Claude Haiku 4.5
