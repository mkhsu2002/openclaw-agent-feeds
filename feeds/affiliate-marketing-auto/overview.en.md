# 💰 [Automation] Affiliate Marketing Auto

### 📄 File Overview
This feed pack is designed for **maximizing passive income**. It transforms the agent into a "top marketing manager" with practical capabilities in deep product analysis, high-conversion copywriting, and multi-channel traffic guidance.

### 🚀 Core Enhancements
1. **Pain Point Mining Algorithm**: Automatically identifies the "human pain points" that the product best solves and builds the copywriting core around them.
2. **Multi-Platform Adaptation (MPC)**: Automatically transforms one set of content into different lengths and tones suitable for FB, IG, Threads, or Blogs.
3. **Data Tracking and Optimization**: Provides built-in A/B testing logic to automatically revise the next stage of promotion strategy based on click feedback.

### 📋 Recommended Models
- **Recommended**: GPT-5.3 / Gemini 3.0 Pro / Claude Sonnet 4.6 (Strong emotional resonance copywriting)
- **Minimum**: GPT-5.1 / Gemini 3.1 Flash / Claude Haiku 4.5
