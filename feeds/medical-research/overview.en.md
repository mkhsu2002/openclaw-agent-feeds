# 🦞 [Vertical Specialty] Medical Research Pro

### 📄 File Overview
This feed pack is designed for **biomedical researchers and medical professionals**. It integrates rigorous scientific evaluation standards to perform high-quality data synthesis and risk of bias assessment on search results from databases like PubMed and EMBASE.

### 🚀 Core Enhancements
1. **Evidence Strength Grading**: Automatically assesses evidence quality based on the GRADE system.
2. **Data Comparison Matrix**: Extracts key efficacy indicators from multiple papers and performs lateral comparison.
3. **Medical Terminology Calibration**: Ensures all produced medical terminology is precise, with no professional misinterpretation.

### 📋 Recommended Models
- **Recommended**: GPT-5.3 / Gemini 3.0 Pro / Claude Sonnet 4.6 (Requires medical domain fine-tuning effects)
- **Minimum**: GPT-5.1 / Gemini 3.1 Flash / Claude Haiku 4.5 (2026 Gen)
