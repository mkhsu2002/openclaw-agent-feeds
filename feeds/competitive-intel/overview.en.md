# 🤺 [Business Intelligence] Competitive Intel Agent

### 📄 File Overview
This feed pack is designed for **staying ahead of competitor movements**. It can automatically monitor competitors' product updates, marketing strategy changes, and public financial report contents, ensuring you always maintain a competitive edge.

### 🚀 Core Enhancements
1. **Feature Comparison**: Automatically generates charts comparing your features with those of your competitors.
2. **Pricing Monitoring and Alerting**: Automatically tracks competitors' pricing strategy transitions and proposes corresponding promotion or price increase suggestions.
3. **Marketing Strategy Deconstruction**: Reverse-engineers competitors' traffic acquisition channels through their public advertisements and posts.

### 📋 Recommended Models
- **Recommended**: GPT-5.3 / Gemini 3.0 Pro / Claude Sonnet 4.6 (Strong long-text comparison capabilities)
- **Minimum**: GPT-5.1 / Gemini 3.1 Flash / Claude Haiku 4.5
